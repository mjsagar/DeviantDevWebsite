Overview

DIY AI ("the App") & CLEAN IT AI ("the App") are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information.

Information We Collect

The App does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini AI: Processes user queries to generate DIY guidance
  • YouTube API: Retrieves video tutorials related to DIY tasks
  • Groq AI: Backup AI service for generating guidance
  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements

Each service operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube Terms
  • Groq Privacy Policy

Data Storage

The App stores API keys locally on your device using iOS Keychain for security. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 18 years and older due to the nature of DIY tasks and safety requirements.
