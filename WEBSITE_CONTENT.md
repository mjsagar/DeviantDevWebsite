## FAQ Section

### Is SwaggerEditor free?
Yes! Guest mode is completely free with no account required. Professional and Enterprise plans add cloud storage and team features.

### Do I need to install anything?
No. SwaggerEditor runs entirely in your browser. Just visit the website and start editing.

### Can I use it offline?
Yes, in guest mode. Your specs are stored locally in your browser.

### What's the difference between guest mode and logged-in mode?
Guest mode stores data locally in your browser. Logged-in mode stores data on our servers, allowing access from any device and team collaboration.

### Do you support OpenAPI 2.0 (Swagger)?
Currently we support OpenAPI 3.0. OpenAPI 2.0 support is coming soon.

### Can I import my existing specs?
Yes! Import from files, URLs, or paste directly into the editor.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. We use JWT authentication, encrypted connections, and isolated storage per user. Guest mode data never leaves your browser.

### Can I self-host SwaggerEditor?
Yes! SwaggerEditor is open source. Deploy to your own servers with Docker.

### What authentication methods do you support?
Email/password, OAuth 2.0, and SSO (SAML, Google, GitHub, Okta, Auth0, Azure AD).

### Do you offer support?
Email support for Professional plans, priority support for Enterprise plans.
